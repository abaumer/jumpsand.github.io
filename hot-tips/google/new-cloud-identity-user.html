How to setup a Free cloud identity user

When you already have Google workspace, or at least kinda, and need to share access with someone to help do adminy things

First
The Google instructions: Start here >


Want more tips?

  1. Login to your google workspace and go to Billing
  2. Click on "Get more services".
    It'll look like this (unless they changed it.)
  3. Click "Get Started" in the Cloud Identity Free box
  4. Then click "Get Started" on the next page too.
  5. Click "Checkout" on the next page under Review your payment plan.
    It's free remember?
  6. Then click "Place order" on the Checkout page
    Still free, not sure why we click all this.
  7. Click "Review License Settings" on the Thanks for your purchase page
  8. Then click "Assign Licenses" on the License Settings page.
  9. Now click "Add new user" towards the top of the Users page.
  10. And fill in the new user info.
    Then click "Add new user"
  11. You should get a page like this!
    If not, uh oh.

    Click on Copy Password, and paste that into an email to send to us or whoever.
    Or into a text message or Signal message, or just save it somewhere.
  12. Now click on "Preview and Send"
    You'll get a box like this.
    Add our real email in there (or the personal email for whoever you're creating this for.)
    Check the box for "Send a copy to myself" (you never know)
    And click "SEND"
  13. Now click on "Done"
    Then it might ask you to save the password again. Do that if you didn't already.
    Then click Done.
  14. Now you should have your new User in the list of users!
    Click on the new user name to edit the settings
  15. On the User page, click the section titled "Admin roles and privileges"
  16. Check the toggle switch for "Super Admin"
    That is if you trust the person or us, if it's us.
    Then click "Save".
  17. Now you've done it.
    Let the person know they have admin access now.
  18. Final note.
    Also let the new admin person know they should check on their user settings to confirm they are not taking up a license for Workspace. Sometimes your settings will automatically add new users to the workspace license, but your new Super Admin person can adjust that for you so that their account won't have a monthly cost.
Published March 2023
